Meet the Team

Brad Barker, DDS

General Dentist

A Houston native, Dr. Barker earned his dental degree at the University of Texas Health Science Center. He stays up-to-date with of the latest technology and practices in dentistry at the prestigious Las Vegas Institute of Advanced Dental Studies to provide the latest technology and techniques, ensuring the best care for you and your family.

Dr. Barker is a member of the American Dental Association, the Texas Dental Association, the American Association of Cosmetic Dentistry, the Texas Academy of General Dentistry, the Academy of Laser Dentistry, and the Academy of General Dentistry.

Kristy

Dental Hygienist

Kristy

Born and raised in the Houston area, Kristy recently moved to the Katy area. She graduated from the University of Texas at Austin with a Bachelor of Science in Nutrition. She has always had a passion for beautiful smiles, so she decided to pursue dentistry and later graduated from the University of Texas Health Science Center, School of Dentistry in Dental Hygiene. She enjoys getting to know her patients and focuses on being as gentle as possible, while providing the most thorough cleaning. Through education, she enjoys seeing your smiles improve over time.
In her free time, Kristy enjoys spending time with her husband and newborn daughter.

Gum Disease


Gum or Periodontal disease is treated by carefully removing the bacteria and substances that form in the pockets around the teeth. The removal of this material occurs on a microscopic level and requires great skill. Our dental team has had advanced training to effectively remove all of the bacteria.
