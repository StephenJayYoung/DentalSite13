Meet the Doctors

Jason Peacock, DDS

Dr. Jason Peacock looks forward to meeting you and working with you to achieve your dental health goals. He received his doctorate of dental surgery degree from the Texas A&M Baylor College of Dentistry and then completed three years of prosthodontic training and a Master of Science at the Ohio State University. He stays up-to-date on the latest dental procedures, surgical techniques, and oral pathology. He is always expanding his level of knowledge to better serve his patients' needs.

When not in the office, Dr. Peacock enjoys spending free-time with his wife and four children doing things in the outdoors.


Rodney Burdette, DDS

Dr. Rodney Burdette enjoys the long-term relationships he's developed with many of his patients and finds it really rewarding to have helped them to be healthier and enjoy the benefits of having a beautiful smile. He graduated from the Georgetown University School of Dentistry with a Doctorate of Dental Surgery and yearly completes additional education courses to keep up-to-date on the latest developments in dentistry to better serve his patients. Dr. Burdette is a member of the Academy of General Dentistry, the New Hampshire Dental Society, and the Concord Dental Society.

During his free time, Dr. Burdette enjoys spending time with his two kids, their spouses and his nine grandchildren. He also enjoys alpine and cross-country skiing, kayaking, swimming and all forms of cycling. He also volunteers with the Friends Program and Adaptive Skiing Program. A Vietnam War veteran, Dr. Burdette spent five years in the Marines after graduating from college.


Meet the Team

Lee

A Registered Dental Hygienist, Lee has worked for Riverview Dental since 2001 and enjoys getting to know her patients and giving them the best care possible. She appreciates that the whole team values patient safety and health by utilizing state-of-the-art equipment, materials, infection control procedures, and techniques.

When Liz isn't caring for her patients, she enjoys spending time with her husband playing tennis, cooking, traveling, kayaking and playing golf. We have four adult children and one grand-child and are blessed to see them often.

Misty

A Certified Dental Assistant, Misty makes sure the doctor has everything he needs when working with the patient. Her focus is patient comfort and education and she finds it rewarding to help patients realize their dental health goals and educate them about the process and update them about their progress.

Misty is married to her husband Chris and they have two children and an English bulldog named Emmy. She and her family are very active in their church and the community and when not serving her patients, she's often volunteering for school or church functions. Misty dances competitively, gardens, and creates artwork and loves enjoying the great outdoors.

Robyn

A Certified Dental Assistant, Robyn is blessed to see a patient from the beginning all the way to the end result. She finds it really rewarding to see a person's face when their treatment is complete. As an office facilitator, Robyn answers the phone, schedules appointments, researches insurance to assist her patients, reviews financing, and coordinates treatments.

Robyn is married to her high school sweatheart and has two sons and a very spoiled dog. As a family, they love to go boating in the nice weather and look forward to their family movie night. She's also an active member of her church and likes to make hand-stamped cards.

Erica

As a Certified Dental Assistant, Erica makes sure the doctors have everything they need for the procedure that's being performed and that her patients are comfortable at all times and that questions are being answered and they are satisfied with their treatment progress. Erika enjoys making patients happy and introducing them to the family atmosphere at Riverview Dental.

Erika enjoys camping, fishing, hiking, hunting and four-wheeling with her fiancee and her three-year-old yellow lab when she's not spending time serving her patients.

Liz

As a Certified Dental Assistant, her attention is focused on making sure her patients are comfortable throughout their visit. Many people have anxiety or fears when it comes to the dentist and her goal is to help alleviate those fears and make her patients as comfortable as possible during their treatments.

Liz was born and raised in New Hampshire and enjoys all the outdoors have to offer, no matter the season. Whether it is going for a hike or to the beach during the summer months or snowboarding in the winter. Her family shares their home with two dogs, a cat, and a bunny.

Erin

As a Registered Dental Hygienist, Erin provides gentle cleanings, sealants, periodontal charting and other preventive care for her patients. She loves the calm, friendly environment at Riverview Dental and seeks to make her patients feel at ease while performing hygiene.

When not in the office, Erin spends time with her husband volunteering within our state's refugee community teaching bible classes, since both she and her husband speak swahili. She also loves to mountain bike, read, and travel.

Joy

After spending seventeen years as a dental hygienist, Joy has a great depth of knowledge about dentistry. She enjoys conversations with patients both in person and on the phone, scheduling appointments, verifying insurance benefits, and helping patients with questions.

Joy is has been married for ten years and has three beautiful girls, all who love dance. She also loves cheerleading, traveling, and coaching soccer.

Periodontal Therapy
